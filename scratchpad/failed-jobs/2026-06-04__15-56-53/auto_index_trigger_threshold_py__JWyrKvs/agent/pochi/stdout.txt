## Module structure

**`get_indexed_table()`** — zero-argument factory that connects to LanceDB at `LANCEDB_URI` and returns an `IndexedTable`.

**`IndexedTable`** — wrapper with two public methods:

- **`add_rows(rows)`** — appends rows to the `vectors` table, then iterates through the sorted threshold list and, for every threshold T where `prev_count < T ≤ new_count`, calls `_rebuild_index(new_count)`.
- **`search(vec, k)`** — delegates to `table.search(vec).distance_type("cosine").limit(k).to_list()`, works both before and after index creation.

**`_rebuild_index(current_row_count)`** — calls `Table.create_index(metric="cosine", index_type="IVF_PQ", num_partitions=max(8, int(sqrt(count))), num_sub_vectors=8, replace=True)` then blocks on `Table.wait_for_index(["vector_idx"], timeout=datetime.timedelta(seconds=300))` before writing the audit log.

**`_write_audit_log(row_count)`** — creates the `index_build_log` table with an explicit PyArrow schema (`row_count_at_build: int64`, `num_partitions: int64`, `ts: string`) on first use; appends to it on subsequent calls.
