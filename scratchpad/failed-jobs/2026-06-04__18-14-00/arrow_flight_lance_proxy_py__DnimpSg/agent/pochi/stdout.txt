### What the file does

1. **Opens the existing database** â€” connects to `/home/user/flight_proxy/lancedb` and opens the `documents` table (read-only; the data is never touched).

2. **`LanceDBFlightServer(FlightServerBase)`** â€” the sole Flight RPC implemented is `do_get`:

3. **Binds to `grpc://0.0.0.0:8815`** via `server.serve()`.
