Maximal Marginal Relevance (MMR) is a classic post-retrieval re-ranking technique that trades off relevance against diversity: starting from a candidate pool, it iteratively picks the document that maximises ``` score(d) = lambda * sim(q, d) - (1 - lambda) * max_{d' in selected} sim(d, d') ``` where `sim` is cosine similarity.
