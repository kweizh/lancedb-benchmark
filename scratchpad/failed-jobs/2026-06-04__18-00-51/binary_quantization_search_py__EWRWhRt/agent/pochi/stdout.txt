## Key design decisions

- **`num_sub_vectors=12`** satisfies the `{8, 12}` requirement while being slightly less destructive than 8 sub-vectors, giving the refine step more correct candidates to work with.
- **`refine_factor=100`** asks LanceDB to retrieve the top `k × 100 = 1000` PQ candidates and re-score them by exact FP32 L2 before returning the final `k`. This is the standard way to recover recall without loosening the compressed index.
- **Brute-force ground truth** reloads all FP32 vectors from the table via `table.to_arrow()` and computes L2 in NumPy — fully independent of the ANN path.
- **Idempotent**: `_build_table` drops the table before recreating it, so two consecutive runs produce an identical, freshly-indexed table.
